Kosgoda Turtle Conservation and Research Center

The Kosgoda Turtle Conservation and Research Centre is a renowned turtle hatchery and conservation facility located in Kosgoda, Sri Lanka. It plays a vital role in protecting sea turtles, raising awareness, and conducting valuable research to ensure the survival of these magnificent creatures.
At the Kosgoda Turtle Conservation and Research Centre, their dedicated team of conservationists and volunteers work tirelessly to safeguard sea turtles and their habitats. The centre focuses on various aspects of turtle conservation, including nesting site protection, hatchery management, and educating local communities and visitors about the importance of turtle conservation.

Key initiatives and activities at the centre include:

  1. Nesting Site Protection: The centre identifies and protects turtle nesting sites along the Sri Lankan coastline. They take measures to secure the nests and monitor them throughout the incubation period to ensure the safe hatching of turtle eggs.
  2. Turtle Hatchery: The Kosgoda centre operates a hatchery where turtle eggs are collected, carefully incubated, and monitored. Once the hatchlings emerge, they are nurtured until they are strong enough to be released into the ocean.
  3. Public Awareness and Education: The centre conducts educational programs and awareness campaigns to promote turtle conservation among local communities, schools, and tourists. Visitors have the opportunity to learn about sea turtles, their life cycles, and the threats they face.
  4. Rehabilitation: In addition to conservation efforts, the centre also provides care and rehabilitation for injured or sick turtles. They offer medical treatment and a safe environment for these turtles to recover before being released back into the wild.
  5. Research and Data Collection: The centre actively engages in research projects to gather important data on sea turtles, their behavior, and migration patterns. This research helps in better understanding and implementing effective conservation strategies.


By visiting the Kosgoda Turtle Conservation and Research Centre, you can witness firsthand the incredible work being done to protect and conserve sea turtles. You will have the opportunity to observe hatchlings, learn about the challenges they face, and contribute to their conservation efforts through donations or volunteer programs.
The Kosgoda Turtle Conservation and Research Centre stands as a beacon of hope for sea turtles in Sri Lanka. Through their dedication and commitment, they inspire individuals and communities to take an active role in preserving these remarkable creatures for future generations.
